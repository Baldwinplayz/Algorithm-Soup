Description

This algorithm will first see which letters are greater without accounting for whether they are capitalized or not. Then the capital letters are accounted for, the capital letters will be considered closer to A in alphabetical order. Similarly, shorter words will be considered closer to A alphabetically, and if the inputted characters are not part of the alphabet then their UNICODE IDs will be used, keep in mind that if non latin characters have capital letters then they should also work. We cannot guarantee perfect resuts when mixing differents scripts together.

Possible uses:

  • Ordering a citation list.
  • Creating dictionaries.
  • Inventory management
  • Data analysis.
