Description

When one is writing a research paper, it is vital for the citations to be provided; this will permit readers and future researchers to go more in-depth into the topic. Currently, there are many different citation formats around the words; some notable ones are: APA, MLA, Harvard, Chicago, etc. The APA format is one of the most popular formats in the world; it was created by the American Psychological Association. Not only that, but it's also a writing format, and one of the requirements of the APA format is to have a citation list organized in alphabetical order to make it easier for the reader to find specific citations.
